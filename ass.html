A cozy evening,friends and family outside the vineyard gisting, talking how the day went, one topic leading to another; this is there usual every evening in the household of Edekere. they were the most peaceful family in the community of ijagba, it was no new thing that most family in the look up to them.
while the discussion and laughter was going on, wife of the eldest son rush out from the hut adjacent where the family was sitted; That's okaifo's (the youngest son) hut. with a big grin on her face, she announced that okaifo wife has given birth to a bouncing baby boy, the whole vineyard lit up, one could sense the joy and jubilations even if they were miles away. every one hailed okaifo, the kids and other wifes rush in to congratulate the latest mother in town while the eldest requested for more palm wine to celebrate this great news

************* ********

it was the 8th day since the birth of okaifo son, this is the day family and friends come together to celebrate. one of the most prominent culture of this community is to name baby on the 8th day after birth. the family of Edekere threw a big party to celebrate the naming of the new born. Mid-celebration, the music was paused to get the attention of everyone present, it was time to announce the name of the precious son; okaifo and his wife came forward with the baby, everyone clapped and cheered, followed by a sudden silence, okaifo announced the name of the baby to everyone from the list he was holding names include:

  • ijoba
  • seyi
  • victor
  • the great
